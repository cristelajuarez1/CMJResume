1. Executive Summary

  • Problem: favoritism, unequal workload, organizational vulnerability
  • Impact: financial losses, security risks, reputational damage
  • Proposed Solution: analytics system to monitor and address risks
  • Expected Benefits: improved decision-making, efficiency, security

2. Organizational Context

  • Current Situation: how the company operates today
  • Factors Enabling Favoritism: lack of merit-based processes
  • Consequences: vulnerable and insecure environment

3. Problem Description & Evidence

  • Role assignment without technical criteria
  • Emotional manipulation & exploitation of vulnerable profiles
  • Institutional cover-ups
  • Retaliation against whistleblowers
  • Demotivation, high turnover, burnout
  • Operational, legal, and reputational impact

4. Key Analysis Conclusions

  • Structural risk: systemic problem, not individual
  • Financial losses: direct and indirect
  • Security vulnerabilities
  • Talent loss and demotivation
  • Loss of client trust
  • Risk of legal actions and penalties

5. Justification for Data-Driven Analysis

  • Why use data analytics
  • KPIs: workload, turnover, productivity, errors, security, reputation
  • Converts abstract issues into quantifiable business risks

6. Current Workflow Diagram

Graphical representation of current processes affected by favoritism and cover-ups, highlighting risk points.

7. Proposed Solution (System Analytics)

  • Improved workflow with merit-based role assignment
  • Dashboards: workload, turnover, productivity, security
  • Early warning system to detect risk patterns
  • Integration with tools: Power BI, SQL, Tableau, internal surveys

8. KPI Dashboard (Mockup)

  • Workload distribution
  • Talent turnover
  • Critical errors
  • Client trust trends

  • Executive Summary
  • Structured Document (Annex 1)
  • Flowcharts (current & proposed)
  • KPI Dashboard
  • Implementation Plan

10. Expected Benefits

  • Economic savings: reduced losses from turnover, errors, legal issues
  • Stronger organizational security
  • Retention of qualified talent
  • Restored trust with clients and strategic partners
  • Sustained market competitiveness
